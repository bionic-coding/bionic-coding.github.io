## Opus Has a Bad Day
 
Opus 4.8 shipped May 28, 2026. In the 10 or so days leading up to the release I noticed undeniable degradation in the performance of the current verison of Opus at the time.

It's like the model is having a bad day.

My best guess is that when they prep an Opus release an infrastructure change results in degraded performance for the current version. I've suspected this before with Sonnet as well.

## Timing is Everything

The timing is incredibly suspicious. With Kimi K3 weights showing up in the next week, Qwen 3.8 Max in the news, and GPT-5.6 Sol launching the week prior, I have to believe that Anthropic is hoping to release model updates to once again cement itself as the leader in AI.
