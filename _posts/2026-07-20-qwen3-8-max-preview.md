Alibaba previewed **Qwen3.8 Max** on July 19 at the World AI Conference in Shanghai. It's a 2.4-trillion-parameter multimodal model. For now it's a paid preview through Alibaba's own platforms, with open weights promised to follow. Alibaba's pitch is that it's "second only to Fable 5," though there are no published benchmarks behind that yet.

Last week I wrote about [Kimi K3]({% post_url 2026-07-17-this-week-in-ai %}), Moonshot's open-weight release. That's two frontier-scale models out of China in a single week. Kimi's weights ship July 27, and Alibaba says Qwen's are coming too.
